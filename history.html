HISTORY

Established in 1995, Science High began as a humble school with very little resources and functioned with only two classrooms, catering to only 97 students. The school, manned by only a handful of faculty members, faced many drawbacks in their early time. However, as their population grew years later, the school was given additional facilities and, in turn, further gave its students an encouraging and nurturing second home. The school’s recognition has swelled over time, thanks to the combined help of its faculty and student body. In 2007, Science High ranked first in the National Achievement Test. In 2011, it was awarded the 45th Tagbilaran City Charter Day Award for Best Performing School. Not only for their achievements, but the school was also known to ingrain in their students the importance of practicing the traditions and values they teach—patriotism, humanity, philanthropy, and love for God.
