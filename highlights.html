Cultural Dance

Every Dude and Dudette believes that we deserve to work, study, live, and have fun. We have a set of year-long student-operated activities that let us socialize with other students, unleash our creativity, and practice what we’ve learned inside the classroom.

The best thing about being a Dude or Dudette is that we’re given a generous amount of creative freedom by the faculty as we work on these activities cooperatively. It’s another impressive teaching approach that’s rather uncommon in most schools.

It’s a collaborative effort to make each student’s experience in TSCI worthwhile, and everyone brings something to the table that makes all these successful. It gives us a platform to raise our voices, try new things, and improve on our skills and talents. It also gives us a chance to socialize.
